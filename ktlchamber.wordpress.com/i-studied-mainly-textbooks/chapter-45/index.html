I Studied Mainly Textbooks! Chapter 45. Divine Artifact Sorole (3)

| Previous | Index | Next |

The most agonizing pain a human can experience is known to be causalgia (pain of burning). This is the type of pain mostly felt by people with CRPS (complex regional pain syndrome), and it is literally the pain of being burnt alive, over and over again.

Taking into consideration that the second most painful sensation was that of the body being chopped apart, it can be inferred just exactly how painful being burnt alive could be.

Avenne had lost consciousness after experiencing her own body being burnt.

When she opened her ‘eyes’, many blackened souls were floating around her.

‘Is this the demon realm…?’

She was acquainted with some of the souls here. They had been members of Apocnaty. Though, she had felt slightly awkward with them since they hadn’t been contracted with a demon.
These were all the souls of black mages, as such, this seemed to be the entrance to the demon realm.

– Finally! I have arrived at the demon realm! Kyahahaha, I must make haste and visit Her Majesty immediately! Where must I go in order to meet the queen?

Avenne asked one of her former black mage underlings.

– What the hell, crazy b*tch? Cap’n, girl’s new here, what do we do with’er?

Huh?

Avenne was flustered.

She did not understand the current situation. The puny guy that was once her underling was so rude to her when she was going to be in the good graces of the queen!

-Hohoho! You must be blind! I am the contractor of the queen of dream demons! I’ll play with you as my slave, fufu.

Avenne had an evil grin plastered across her face, but unlike before, no one was fearful of her. This space was no different from the afterlife after all.

– Ay! The master of the room is one with the most math skills! Consider yourself lucky that you don’t have a physical body!

If they were able to touch each other, then she would have been in trouble. After all, she had entered a man’s den.

***

Only after a lot of time had passed did she realize what kind of situation she was in.

According to the other imprisoned souls, she was in a space she could never escape, and the only way to exit was to prove the math formula written on the wall.

-Fermat’s Last Theorem?

This was why ‘one with the most math skills’ was the ‘master of the room’.

Numerous souls were strewn everywhere, exhausted. The current master was a brat named Cain.

‘Abel this damned… whoaaa?’

Avenne suddenly felt that her soul was being sucked somewhere.

Swoosh-
The place her soul arrived at was a new space that was dark and murky.

‘This place?’

Demonic energy was aplenty and sulphur gas sprouted from the ground! This completely matched her imagination of the ‘demon realm’.

Trod trod-
From within the dark demonic energy, a charming lady approached Avenne. She had large black wings, a pointy tail, lewd clothing that covered the barest of skin, and a beautiful face that could not be described with words!

This was the succubus queen that she had wanted to meet so much.

“My daughter. It is nice to finally see you. I had a hard time finding you because you were locked in a strange place. Fufufufu.”

That mad laughter! She was definitely one of the dream demons. Meaning… this was really the demon realm!

-Your Majesty!

Avenne expressed her emotional salutations. She looked like she was about to cry at any moment. Though, she was only a soul and was thus unable to shed tears.

“Hoho, yes. I’ve not seen the situation of the human realm. How is it all going?”

-Please punish me… There’s a setback in regards to the plans. The ‘Dark Mages’ group was almost annihilated. But we ‘Demon Worshippers’ and the other two groups are still intact.

“Okay. How is the dimensional crack coming along?”

-The dimensional crack? Oh! Is Your Majesty referring to plan B? We’ve infiltrated the Gaia Church and are looking for the location of the key. If we manage to locate the hidden ruins of the Zesus church, we should be able to get our hands on Sorole. Fufufu.

“The divine artifact Sorole?”

-Yes! Lord Demon God has told us that he would be able to open a passage to the demon realm as long as we had that. If that happens, you will be able to descend to this place with your physical body!

“Hohoho, I rather look forward to that.”
The succubus queen laughed charmingly as though she was looking forward to this.

– We were planning to offer you the human realm with our powers alone… but since the initial plan has failed, the others should have turned to plan B!

“Then do you know where the passageway is opening?”

-As far as I know, they’re widening the current dimensional crack.

“Where is this place exactly? Oh, I’m asking because I can’t wait to see it. You can tell me where the general location is in the human realm.”

-My memories were erased, so I do not know either. In the human realm, only Demonik knows of it.

When Avenne’s words finished, the queen suddenly stood up and started stretching.

Crack-!
Bone snapping sounds could be heard from the queen’s waist. It couldn’t sound any more refreshing.

“Ah, my bones hurt from trying to look sexy.”
The queen’s tone of words and the mood around her suddenly changed.

Swoosh-
The black demonic energy disappeared and the surrounding environment could be properly seen. The queen’s clothing, looks, and even gender had switched.

– You, you are!
Avenne shouted in a fluster after seeing what happened. The ‘queen’ that had returned to ‘her’ original form was someone she knew.

“Heya. We see again. I hope I never see you again now.”
It was Abel.

– No, no way. All of this was…!?
Avenne’s eyes shook after realizing that all of this was a trap that Abel had set up.

“It’s just as you think. Thanks for the intel. Hahaha!”
Abel laughed heartily.

Avenne had endured all forms of torture in the palace dungeons. She even told them some false information. The only option to get information out of her was to lower her guard before interrogating her.

Abel had transformed using an illusion spell in order to get information out of her. To fool her properly, he even had to imitate her sultry method of speaking.

Thanks to that though, he was able to get some proper intel out of her. Avenne was someone influential even within the ‘Demon Worshippers’ group. Although he couldn’t get the location of the dimensional crack, he had gotten most of the information he needed.

-How did you know about the dimensional crack…?

The fact that there was a crack in the dimension was only known to high members of Apocnaty including her.

How was Abel aware of that? From his knowledge of Sorole as well, she could never in her wildest dreams have imagined that the queen was an imposter.

“I’m always five moves ahead of you.”
Abel spoke with a smile.

“There, that’s all for this visit. Go back and repent.”

Abel opened the door to Schrodinger’s Prison.

-Eek! No! Kyaaaak!….

Swoosh-
Avenne screeched in disbelief of the reality she was in. She was sucked into the prison once again.

When Avenne disappeared, Abel’s leisurely attitude was gone as well.

“I still can’t be at ease…”

He had screwed over all the black mages in the Hela Empire, but there were supposedly three more groups to break. It seemed that this ‘Demonik’ guy seemed to be the strongest guy of the bunch.

‘They’re also aiming for Sorole!’

He didn’t know why they always seemed to cross paths.

He couldn’t let those damned black mages steal it. It was a fortune that one of the key pieces was in his hands. Since they had people planted in the Gaia Church, he couldn’t ask for their cooperation outright either. After all, nothing good would come out of telling them that he possessed one of the keys.

***

Abel had returned to his room after interrogating Avenne. He fell into deep contemplation.

“I should do something about this body.”

From the information he got from Avenne, the group that attacked the emperor was merely one of four groups forming the organization. Despite that, there were two swordmasters in their ranks. Statistically speaking, they likely had 6 more in their ranks. And from what he felt, the power of swordmasters were outright insane.

‘They’re able to cut space apart.’

The power of aura was beyond his imagination. It was able to slash apart his specialty space magic.

If he was to face another swordmaster, or perhaps, even a grand swordmaster in the future, he had to raise the capabilities of his body. If it came down to close quarters combat, he wouldn’t be able to do anything in the state he was currently in.

“I guess I can learn basic movements from Reina…”

Growing muscle and learning footwork to avoid weapons at close quarters were things he could learn with enough time. However, he needed a secret weapon.

After all, he wouldn’t be able to overcome the limitations of a human no matter how hard he trained his body. He wasn’t a knight.

However, one positive side was that his reflexes were very good since his brain had improved. If he had the physique to support it, he would be able to stand on equal footing with those large American football players.

“Haa, I hate exercise though…….”

But, he had to do it in order to erase those black mages from existence.

If he earnt a splendid body through exercise, he would have an easier time dodging weapons. Though… it probably still wouldn’t work against a swordmaster.

He needed something that was above that.

Aura could rip apart space, so he needed something to counter that.
Or, he had to think up a smart method to delay for more time.

As such, Abel had come up with ‘Reflexive Spell.’

What if he could cast a spell with the movements of his physical body alone without any computation from his brain? That would suffice for close quarters combat.

‘Casio! Since the synchro rate is 99 percent, you can repeatedly calculate the precise coordinates of my fingertips, right?’

[It is possible.]

Moving the body meant that the coordinates changed constantly. However, the ‘tip of his fingers’ was a part of his body.

Since the synchro rate between the two was 99%, Casio was able to process the coordinates of Abel’s fingertips real-time.

‘If I extend my finger, apply the compression spell at my fingertips!’

[Yes master!]

Abel’s thought process went like this: He would extend his finger and compress the space around his fingertips. Then, space would expand in the direction of his fingertips after an instant, compressing the space towards the front. It would then trigger a chain of compressions and explode upon contact with a foreign object.

Namely, airpang!

(T/N: originally ‘Gongipang’ which would translate into ‘airpop’ but I think airpang sounds better.)

Since it was simple compression magic and the spell would start from his fingertips, there was no need to set a proper coordinate for the target. In other words, he would be able to use this spell without any thinking, reflexively.

As for the actual spell?

Abel was currently in his secret room right now.

He stood upright with his feet shoulder-width apart. He shaped his hand like a pistol.

“Hahp!”
Abel pointed at a wall far away with his right index finger.

Swoosh-Boom!
The space around the wall exploded and a huge crater was formed. The chain reaction occurred so quickly that it felt like the wall exploded as soon as he pointed towards it. It was slower than light, but the speed was similar to that of a bullet.

‘What the!’

Abel was shocked by his own results.

It was like he was a walking machine gun!

His new technique practically made him a walking strategic weapon.

Back on Earth, firearms used bullets that had mass. However, Abel’s airpang crunched and imploded space itself. This meant that his ‘bullet’ wouldn’t be influenced by the surrounding environment. Moreover, there was no rebound. The size of the ‘bullet’ was much bigger than standard rounds as well.

In other words, his airpang was more destructive and more accurate.

Abel seemed excited with his new toy and started pointing his fingers everywhere.

“Bang bang bang!”

Boom! Boom! Boom!
Loud impacts occurred and all the places he pointed at exploded, sending small shards of debris scattering.

“Fuu… It’s high noon.”
(T/N: Apparently an Overwatch thing… but I’ve never played it so…)

After the explosion, Abel tried to make himself look cool by blowing on his fingertips. It was as though he was an outlaw in a cowboy movie.
